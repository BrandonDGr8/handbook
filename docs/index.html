What is a Dev Team?

  • Builds projects for the Hackathon
  • Teaches people real-world programming
  • Builds a community

Real-world Programming

Being in a Dev Team will:

  • teach you about dealing with users.
  • give you experience with getting requirements and managing projects.
  • teach you best practices.

Building a Community

A good Dev Team will:

  • keep the project-building tradition alive.
  • expose the CS community to other communities everywhere.
